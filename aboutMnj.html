Manoj Jauhari, born on 21st Feb 1972 at Kanpur, did his schooling at Avadi, Madras (now known as Chennai). From 1st standard to 12th standard, his schooling was at Ordnance Clothing Factory (OCF) School. In 1989, he moved to Maharashtra to pursue engineering in computer science from College Of Engineering, Osmanabad. After completing Engineering in 1994, he worked in Chennai for a couple of months for Royal Goan Beach Club (RGBC), selling holiday timeshares to prospective customers who were invited for a detailed presentation every evening being lured to receive a special gift at the end of the presentation. In 1994, Manoj joined Bureau of Data Processing Systems (BDPS) as a trainer for C/C++, DOS and Dbase. In 1995, Manoj moved to Hyderabad to join Syntel Microsystems Pvt Ltd (also known as Digital ATC) as trainer/programmer.

In December 1996, Manoj moved to Bangalore to join SwissSoft India Pvt Ltd as a Software Engineer.
